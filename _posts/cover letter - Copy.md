Dear Hiring Manager,
I am writing to express my interest in the Junior ETL Developer position within your Decision Support and Business Intelligence team. With a strong foundation in data systems, cloud infrastructure, and a recent specialization in Health Informatics, I am excited about the opportunity to contribute to transforming healthcare data into meaningful insights that improve patient care and operational outcomes.

In my recent role as a DevOps Engineer and SME at Kyndryl, I worked extensively with large-scale data environments across Azure and AWS, supporting high-volume transaction systems and maintaining reliable infrastructure. I have hands-on experience writing scripts in Python, analyzing system logs, and troubleshooting complex integration issues—skills that translate directly to building, validating, and maintaining robust ETL pipelines. My work has consistently required attention to data integrity, system reliability, and performance optimization.

Additionally, my experience at Sun Life Financial and Citibank strengthened my ability to investigate data and middleware issues, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and document technical processes clearly. While my primary focus has been DevOps and systems engineering, I have developed a strong working knowledge of SQL-based data handling and relational systems, and I am eager to deepen my expertise in T-SQL, ETL development, and BI tooling such as Power BI or SSIS.

What particularly excites me about this role is the opportunity to work in a healthcare environment. Through my postgraduate studies in Health Informatics at George Brown Polytechnic, I gained firsthand experience in healthcare data standards, electronic medical records, and the critical role data plays in clinical decision-making. I am motivated to apply both my technical and domain knowledge in a mission-driven setting where data directly impacts patient outcomes.

I am a detail-oriented and analytical professional who thrives in collaborative environments and is eager to learn and grow. I am confident that my technical background, combined with my passion for healthcare and willingness to learn, makes me a strong fit for this role.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I would welcome the opportunity to further discuss how I can contribute to your team.

Sincerely,
Ania Skudlarska
